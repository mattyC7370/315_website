Service Page


I worked with our church Clear Lake Presbyterian, and a group called mission discovery on construction projects in Edna, Jamaica (2014)
and Antigua, Guatemala (2016). In jamaica we worked with Jamaican Christian School for the Deaf constructing concrete buildings used for
classrooms, hundreds of special needs children have their educational needs met here. In Guatemala our church
constructed 3 homes and provided an after school program for the youth.
